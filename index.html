Book Critic

Welcome to Book Critic, a site in development!

Have you ever heard an established or new author say that their book is a New York Times Best Seller? Have you ever thought, "How do I choose which best seller I want to read this week"? What if there was an application that allowed you to find the current weeks best Fiction and Non-Fictional sellers and allows you to get a review from real people!

Well, Book Critic makes this possible. Book Critic will give you a list of This weeks Fictional and Non-Fictional best sellers, and connects you to youtube reviews.
